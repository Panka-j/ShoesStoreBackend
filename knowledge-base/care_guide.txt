SHOESSTORE SNEAKER CARE GUIDE

Mesh and Knit uppers (e.g. Nike React, Adidas Ultra Boost):
- Remove laces and insoles before cleaning
- Use a soft brush with mild soap and cold water
- Never machine wash — heat warps the sole and damages cushioning
- Air dry at room temperature, away from direct sunlight
- Stuff with newspaper while drying to maintain shape

Leather uppers (e.g. Air Force 1, Jordan 1 leather):
- Wipe with a damp cloth after every wear to prevent crease buildup
- Apply a leather conditioner every 2-3 months
- Use a crease protector insert to keep the toe box fresh
- For scuffs: dab white toothpaste, scrub gently, wipe clean
- Store with shoe trees to maintain shape

Suede uppers (e.g. Puma Suede, New Balance 574):
- Use a suede brush after every wear to lift the nap
- Apply suede protector spray before first wear — creates a water-resistant barrier
- Never wet suede directly — use a suede eraser for dry stains
- For deep stains: take to a professional cleaner

White rubber soles (all white soles):
- Magic eraser (melamine foam) removes yellowing and scuffs
- Mix baking soda + hydrogen peroxide for stubborn yellowing — apply, wrap in cling film, leave in sunlight 1 hour
- Toothbrush + dish soap for midsole grooves

Storage tips:
- Store in original box or clear plastic sneaker boxes
- Keep silica gel packets inside box to absorb moisture
- Never store in direct sunlight — UV light yellows soles and fades colourways
- Rotate your sneakers — wearing the same pair daily breaks down cushioning faster
- For long-term storage: clean thoroughly, apply crease protectors, and store in a cool dry place
