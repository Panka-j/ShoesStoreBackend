SHOESSTORE STREETWEAR STYLING GUIDE

Air Force 1 (low and high):
- Goes with everything: joggers, jeans, shorts, even formal-casual fits
- Best colourways for beginners: Triple White, Black/White, Colour of the Month drops
- Stack your pants slightly for the cleanest look

Dunks:
- Wear with straight-leg or slightly baggy jeans, cargos, or shorts
- Avoid skinny jeans — Dunks need space to breathe
- Match one colour from the shoe to your top for a cohesive fit

Jordan 1 High:
- The ultimate streetwear staple — fits cargo pants, wide-leg jeans, hoodies, oversized tees
- Tuck your pants INTO the collar for a bold, styled look
- Chicago colourway: match with red or white pieces only

Adidas Samba:
- The 2024 silhouette of the year — goes with everything slim
- Best with straight-cut or tapered jeans, not wide-leg
- Pairs well with vintage and retro aesthetics

New Balance 550:
- Preppy-meets-street — pairs with corduroy pants, straight jeans, polo shirts
- White/Grey colourway is the most versatile

Seasonal tips:
- Monsoon: avoid suede and canvas — go for leather or synthetic uppers
- Summer: light colourways and mesh uppers keep it cool
- Winter: darker colourways, leather uppers, high silhouettes for protection
