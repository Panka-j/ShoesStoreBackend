SHOESSTORE SIZE GUIDE

General advice:
- Measure your foot length in cm for the most accurate sizing
- If between sizes, go half size up for running shoes, true to size for casual
- Wide feet: go up half a size for most brands

Nike Size Chart (Men's):
US 6 = UK 5.5 = EU 39 = 24 cm
US 7 = UK 6 = EU 40 = 25 cm
US 8 = UK 7 = EU 41 = 26 cm
US 9 = UK 8 = EU 42.5 = 27 cm
US 10 = UK 9 = EU 44 = 28 cm
US 11 = UK 10 = EU 45 = 29 cm
US 12 = UK 11 = EU 46 = 30 cm
Nike tip: Air Max and React styles run half a size large. Dunk runs true to size.

Adidas Size Chart (Men's):
US 6 = UK 5.5 = EU 38.5 = 23.5 cm
US 7 = UK 6.5 = EU 40 = 25 cm
US 8 = UK 7.5 = EU 41.5 = 26 cm
US 9 = UK 8.5 = EU 42.5 = 27 cm
US 10 = UK 9.5 = EU 44 = 28 cm
US 11 = UK 10.5 = EU 45 = 29 cm
Adidas tip: Ultra Boost runs half size small. Stan Smith and Samba run true to size.

Jordan Size Chart (Men's):
Jordans use Nike sizing. See Nike chart above.
Jordan tip: Jordan 1 runs half size large. Jordan 4 and 11 run true to size.

New Balance Size Chart (Men's):
US 7 = EU 40 = 25 cm
US 8 = EU 41.5 = 26 cm
US 9 = EU 42.5 = 27 cm
US 10 = EU 44 = 28 cm
US 11 = EU 45 = 29 cm
New Balance tip: 550 and 990 run true to size. Available in D (standard) and 2E (wide) widths.

Puma Size Chart (Men's):
Puma runs approximately half a size small compared to Nike.
Add half size when converting from Nike sizing.

Women's sizing:
Generally subtract 1.5 sizes from men's US sizing to get women's US sizing.
Example: Men's US 8 = Women's US 9.5
